About Us

A Strategic Growth Centre in Ntoroko

Karugutu Town Council is a growing urban settlement in Western Uganda, Ntoroko District. Elevated to autonomous status in 2010 and fully recognized under Statutory Instrument 2016 No. 87 on 29th June 2016.

Bordered by the Toro-Semliki National Park, Karugutu serves as a gateway to wildlife tourism and sustainable natural resource management.
